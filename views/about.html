About Us

MS Dharani was born on April 7, 2004, in Srivilliputtur. He hails from a family of weavers, with his father, K. Madasamy, and mother, M. Saraswati, also being involved in the weaving profession. Dharani has an elder brother, M. Muthukumar, who is a graduate in Electrical and Communication Engineering.

MS Dharani's educational journey has been marked by dedication and accomplishments. He completed his school education at Thiyagaraja Hr. Sec. School in Srivilliputtur. Later, he pursued a Bachelor's in Computer Applications (BCA) at Sri Kaliswari College in Sivakasi, with plans to continue his studies with an MCA degree.

However, MS Dharani's talents extend beyond the academic sphere. He is a passionate musician, excelling in keyboard playing. His musical journey includes participating in various intercollegiate meets, where he has consistently demonstrated his skill. Notably, he secured the 1st prize in an instrumental playing competition at Parvathi's Art and Science College in Dindigul and the 2nd prize in keyboard playing at Mannar Thirumalai Nayakar College in Madurai. In his college, Sri Kaliswari College, he showcased his musical prowess during the annual day performance and claimed the 1st prize in a talent competition for keyboard playing.

MS Dharani's interests go beyond music. He is a keen learner of programming languages and is passionate about web development. His achievements include success in coding competitions, websiting, and quiz contests. Notably, he has also ventured into the world of content creation with two YouTube channels. "IT'S ME TAMILAN" is an entertainment channel with nearly 100,000 subscribers, while "MS Dharani" is dedicated to showcasing his musical talents and has garnered over 20,000 subscribers.

MS Dharani's life is a fusion of artistic creativity and technological innovation, where music and programming harmoniously coexist. With a bright educational path ahead and a thriving presence in the world of music and content creation, Dharani is a multi-talented individual with a promising future.
